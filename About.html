My Beginning

I started my journey into technology with a simple curiosity about how software actually work behind the scenes. What began as experimentation by request of a mentor, quickly turned into a genuine passion for building things that people can interact with.

Learning & Growth

As a self-taught developer, I’ve learned to be patient, persistent, and adaptable. I focused on understanding the fundamentals of HTML, layout design, responsive websites, and later expanded into Python for problem solving and automation.

Every project I build teaches me something new — whether it’s improving usability, structuring cleaner code, or thinking more critically about user experience.

What I Care About

I value clean design, clarity, and purpose. I believe websites should not only look good, but also feel intuitive and of course, work. I enjoy creating digital experiences that are simple, professional, and effective.

Looking Ahead

I’m continuously developing my skills and taking on new challenges. My goal is to keep building meaningful projects, collaborate with others, and grow into a well-rounded developer who creates work they can be proud of.
